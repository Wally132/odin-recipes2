Street Tacos

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef - 20 pounds
  • Onions - 50 pounds
  • Tortillas - 1 metric ton
  • Salsa - a lot
  • Cilantro (optional)
  • Taco seasoning of your choice

Steps

  1. Cook the meat to your liking.
  2. Put the torilla on the griddle for a few seconds.
  3. Put the meat on top of the torilla.
  4. Put the other stuff on if you want.
