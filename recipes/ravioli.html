Octruple Cheese Ravioli

My Signiture Dish

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • 8 Cheeses of your choice
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parsley
  • More Cheese
  • 20 sticks of butter

Steps

  1. Put the cheese in the pasta.
  2. Put a layer of pasta over the first pasta.
  3. Cook it in the butter and heavy cream.
  4. Put more cheese on top.
  5. Set the oven to 800 degrees and cook for an additional 60 minutes.
  6. Slurp it up.
